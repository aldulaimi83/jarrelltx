Frequently asked questions

What to know before we begin.

How long does a website take?

Timing depends on scope and how quickly business content is available. A practical schedule is provided before work begins.

Do I own my website?

Ownership, access, and any ongoing services are documented clearly in the project agreement before work begins.

Can you update my existing website?

Yes. We can review the current site and discuss whether focused improvements or a larger rebuild is the practical option.

Can you help with my Google Business Profile?

We can assist with organizing business information and provide profile guidance. Google controls profile verification, approval, and platform decisions.

Do you provide hosting?

We can discuss appropriate hosting and domain options and explain ongoing costs. The recommended setup depends on the project.

Do you work only with Jarrell businesses?

Jarrell is the local focus. Nearby Central Texas businesses may also contact us to discuss their needs.

What information do you need to begin?

We typically need your services, service area, contact details, existing website or profile links, photos, and the main action you want customers to take.
