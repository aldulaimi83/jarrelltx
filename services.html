Frequently asked

Good questions, clearly answered.

How long does a website take?

Timing depends on scope and how quickly content is ready. A focused site can move faster than a multi-page or custom-tool project. You’ll get a realistic schedule before work starts.

Can you work with my existing website?

Yes. We can first look at what is worth keeping, what needs to change and whether a redesign or focused improvement makes more sense.

Do I own the finished website?

Ownership and any ongoing services are made clear in the project agreement before work begins.

Is ongoing support required?

No. Support can be added where useful, but it is not presented as a surprise requirement.
