Privacy Policy

Last updated July 18, 2026

Information you provide

When you email JarrellTX.co or prepare a request through a website form, you may provide your name, email address, phone number, business name, business details, and message. The main contact form is processed by FormSubmit.co, while the directory submission form uses an email-application fallback.

Business listing submissions

Business listing submissions may include public-facing business information such as an address, phone number, website, social links, business description, and services. Information is reviewed before publication. Submission does not guarantee publication.

How information is used

Information is used to respond to requests, discuss services, review directory submissions, verify business details, and correct published information. JarrellTX.co does not sell submitted contact information.

Server and analytics data

The site is hosted through GitHub Pages. Basic server logs may be processed by the hosting provider as part of operating and securing the service. JarrellTX.co does not currently load a separate analytics or advertising tracker.

External links and maps

The site links to services such as Google Maps, LinkedIn, email applications, and business websites. Those services have their own privacy practices. Embedded Google Maps content may connect to Google when the map loads.

Corrections and deletion requests

To request a correction or removal of submitted information or a directory listing, email info@jarrelltx.co. Provide enough detail to identify the relevant request or business information.

Policy updates

This policy may be updated as the website and directory develop. The date at the top identifies the latest published revision.
