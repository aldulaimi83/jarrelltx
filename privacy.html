What we collect

If you contact us by email or through a future connected form, we may receive the information you provide, such as your name, email, optional phone number, business name and message.

How we use it

We use information to respond to questions, service requests and directory listing requests. We do not claim to collect information from a form that has not transmitted it.

Preview profiles are clearly identified and are not representations of real listed businesses. Before real business information is published, it should be supplied or confirmed by the business.

The site is designed to work without third-party tracking scripts. If analytics or other tools are added, this policy should be updated to describe them.
