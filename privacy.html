Privacy Policy

This is a straightforward policy for a small local business website and directory.

What we collect

When you submit a contact or review form, we may receive the information you enter, such as your name, email address, phone number, business name, and message.

How we use it

We use form submissions to respond to questions, review website requests, listing updates, and other service inquiries.

Directory information

Directory data on this site may include demo listings while the site is growing. Business owners should verify any information before relying on it.

Analytics and cookies

Future analytics tools should be added carefully and disclosed clearly if they are enabled. The site is designed to work without third-party tracking scripts.

Contact and updates

If you want to update, claim, or remove a listing, use the contact page. If you want your submitted contact information removed where practical, mention that in your request.
